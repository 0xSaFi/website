GtkGL.Rotation

This class describes a rotation about an axis in Euler angles. The rotation values may be positive or negative. The constructor takes as its arguments a GtkGL.Rotation.Direction value (either Clockwise or CounterClockwise), and X, Y and Z rotation values in degrees. The normalized range is [0 .. 359].
